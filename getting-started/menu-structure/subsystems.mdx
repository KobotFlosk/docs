---
title: "Subsystems"
---

## Audio
Toggles if the unit can receive IM's and see Local Chat.

## Unit Comm.
More refinement of RLV toggling Radar / GPS, or even IM's and Chat.

## Core
Toggles if the units mind is on or off (Refer to CORE if the mind is off).

## Optics
Toggles the units vision on or off.

## QTT (Quantum Tesseract)
Toggles the units teleport capability on or off.

## Servos
Toggles the units servos: **FULL** / **HALF** / **HALT**.

## Manage
Change the units color, gender, you name it! Personalize your unit.
