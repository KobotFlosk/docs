title: "KOR HUD"

The HUD comes with every purchase of the Controller or BYOC, it is **FREE**!

## Features
*   **Toggle Helper**: Click on the question mark (?) on the HUD to see what each button does. Click anywhere on the HUD to close it.
*   **Menu Bar Visibility**: Use commands `!hide hud` and `!show hud` to control the visibility of the menu bar.
*   **[User Interface](/general-information/kor-hud/user-interface)**: A simple dashboard for managing your unit.
