### Increasing Time

<Note>
By default every KOR Echo will be preloaded to 1 day capacity.
</Note>

You can either click on the controller itself, or you can run the command **!menu** *(You will need to click on devices within the menu)* or **!menu devices** to bring you straight there.

Look for the device named '**Internal Power Cell**' once located click on the device.

Within this menu you will have the option to **Adjust Power** inside this menu you will be presented with this dialog.

<Frame caption="Power Capacity Adjuster">
  <img src="/images/gitbook/power-cell-adjuster.png" />
</Frame>

<Note>
If increasing the capacity beyond the original 24 hours, you will be required to charge. There is no maximum to how long your controller can last.
</Note>

## Must Knows

This controller has **OPTIONAL** position saving which must be enabled by running the command [**!prsxyz**](/getting-started/command-reference) after booting and positioning your controller. Otherwise if you choose to not save the position you are free to move it about your avatar with no issue.

* This controller has a **NON-REMOVABLE** Plasma Cell.
* This being a plasma based controller electric chargers are not effective at charging this controller to 100%. You will only be able to reach an approx. 70% (68.6% actual) of charge with **ANY** electric charger.
* Plasma based chargers from K.I.S.S are required for this controller to be charged to 100%.
