### New Commands

| Command | Usage | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **vent** | `!helix vent` | Manually vent the controller, expelling heat within a 5-meter radius and affecting nearby KOR units. |
| **size** | `!helix size` | Saves the current resize of the controller to ensure normal operation. |

<Warning>
Resize the controller first before running the `!helix size` command to save the new dimensions.
</Warning>

### Color Channels
- **Primary Color:** This is your plasma color. Change it via <code>!color a &lt;color&gt;</code> or through the **Manage** menu.
- **Secondary Color:** This is the outer ring and inner processor color. Change it via <code>!color b &lt;color&gt;</code> or through the **Manage** menu.

### Side-Nubs
The controller has optional side-nubs that can be removed at the user's own discretion.

### Must Knows
- **Optional Position Saving:** Enable by running `!prsxyz` after booting and positioning your controller. Otherwise, you can move it about your avatar freely.
- **Non-Removable Plasma Cell:** This controller uses a built-in plasma cell.
- **Charging:** Electric chargers are not effective at charging this controller to 100%. They will only reach approximately **70%** (68.6% actual). 
- **Runtime:** A 70% charge provides roughly **2 Days, 21 Hours, and 15 minutes** of runtime.
- **Plasma Chargers:** Plasma-based chargers from K.I.S.S. are required to reach a full 100% charge.
