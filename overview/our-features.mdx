### Why choose KOR?

KOR is designed with stability, speed, and user experience in mind. By utilizing a thin-client architecture, we reduce the load on Second Life's simulator and provide features that were previously impossible or unreliable.

### KOR's Features

- **Gridwide Hive Chat:** Enjoy seamless communication with Gridwide Hive Chat. Chat across different regions without being in the same sim, enhancing your connectivity in Second Life.
- **Web Interface:** Manage your system effortlessly with a user-friendly web dashboard. View and change settings, and track your teleport history for a personalized experience.
- **Nanite/ACS Compatibility:** Experience smooth integration with Nanite and ACS products. KOR's compatibility allows for easy swapping between systems, providing flexibility in your choices.
- **Built-in Full-Fledged RLV Relay:** KOR comes with an integrated RLV relay, adding depth to your role-play with advanced control features.
- **Over-the-Air Updating:** Benefit from hassle-free updates. KOR's thin-client system allows for on-the-spot bug fixes and updates, reducing the need for new copies.
- **Script Error Auto Recovery:** KOR's built-in health checks ensure all scripts function smoothly, providing a worry-free experience by automatically addressing errors.
- **Advised Unit Follow Capability:** Enhance your interactions with a sophisticated follow feature, adding realism to your Second Life experience.
- **Support for Customized Housings:** Personalize your experience with BYOC KIT, allowing you to transform any object into a KOR controller.
- **Weather and Thermal Dynamics:** KOR's advanced system tracks environmental factors like fluids and temperature, adding another layer of realism to your virtual world.

<Note>
KOR is ever-evolving, and we believe the best enhancements come from our users' experiences. If you have suggestions or ideas for new features, we're all ears! With our flexible system architecture, we can often integrate user suggestions quickly and efficiently. Share your thoughts, and help us make KOR even better for everyone in Second Life.
</Note>

## Why choose KOR

* **Think Thin:**
  Experience a lightweight, efficient Second Life with KOR. Our system uses minimal scripts and memory, leading to less confusion and a smoother user experience. Embrace thin-client technology for a user-friendly, streamlined approach.
* **Think Smart:**
  KOR is future-proofed with a server-managed architecture, keeping your system up-to-date automatically. Enjoy seamless adaptability, auto-recovery, and enhanced error resilience, ensuring a stable and cutting-edge experience.
* **Think Bold:**
  With KOR, handle high demand effortlessly. Our server-processed system and internal load balancing manage numerous requests per second, ensuring consistent, top-notch performance.
* **Nanite Systems Compatibility:**
  KOR is highly compatible with NS products, offering a smooth transition with about 95% compatibility. Switch for a more reliable experience where your settings are preserved and system malfunctions are minimized.
* **First-ever User-Interface:**
  Navigate with ease using KOR's unique User Interface. Forget complex command memorization and confusing dialogs. Our intuitive UI simplifies your interactions for an effortless Second Life experience.
